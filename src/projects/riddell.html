About

Hi, my name is Joe Cochran. I’m a Full Stack Web Developer living and working in Arroyo Grande, CA. I work primarily with PHP and JavaScript, utilizing Laravel , Angular.js , Node.js and a variety of other tools and languages.

I primarily work with Git for version control, Sass as my CSS preprocessor, and Gulp for asset concatenation and compilation. I’ve also worked with SVN, Mercurial, and Grunt.

The majority of my professional work has been custom web applications written in PHP relying on the Laravel MVC framework. Aside from Laravel, I have created and customized sites built on some of the more popular CMS packages, including Wordpress, Magento, and CS-Cart.
